FAQ

  • How many team members can I invite?

    You can invite as much as you want! Go and tell your friends!

  • What is the maximum file upload size?

    Today the maximum size we can store is 500 mb, but we are going to give more in the future!

  • How do I reset my password?

    Go to settings > security > password > change password. Follow the steps.

  • Can I cancel my subscription?

    Of course you can, just remember to cancel it before the 1st of every month.

  • Do you provide additional support?

    Yes, write us an email to support.frontend@mentor.com with all your issues
